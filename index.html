My Experience At AkiraChix

AkiraChix

AkiraChix is an organization located in Karen, Kenya.
It offers scholarship for young women between 18 and 24 years who are socio-economically disadvantaged.
It is a one-year program and is fully sponsored.
It brings together women from different parts of Africa.

Reporting Day

I joined AkiraChix on 9th February 2024. I was welcomed hospitably from the gate to the Kaya.
We had breakfast with our parents while waiting for all students to arrive.
The hospitality was admirable that even my parent complimented the Campus.

Classes

I was assigned to Lovelace Lab.
The class in session was about Navigating Your Journey.
We talked about

  1. Our Goals
  2. Hobbies and Talents
  3. What we were doing before joining AkiraChix
For a first class, it was engaging and fun.
This is because of the introduction session which was lively.

Accommodation

I was assigned to the room called Kalahari which hosts eight people.
Our room has the following;

    Bathroom with a bathtub
    Cabinets for everyone
    Our own balcony
Summary

My stay at AkiraChix has been good so far.
It is a place fit for studies and away from destruction.
You get a moment to focus on, and build on yourself.
